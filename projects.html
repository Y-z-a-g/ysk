UncopyrightedGenres

I started Leafy Radio, initially named UncopyrightedGenres, back on March 3, 2012. The main goal was to share non-copyright music, offering a collection that could be freely used for various projects. In the early stages, the channel had a different focus and the content from those days was eventually made unlisted, marking a shift in the project's direction. The evolution from there has been a journey of discovering and sharing music in a way that respects artists' rights while providing value to the audience.
